## The Challenge

Bid teams in medical device and healthcare companies face the same problems:

- **Manual document review**: Reading hundreds of pages to extract requirements takes days.
- **Compliance gaps**: Requirements are missed or misinterpreted, leading to disqualification.
- **Pricing guesswork**: Without historical data, pricing decisions are reactive, not strategic.
- **Inconsistent proposals**: Each bid manager formats proposals differently, weakening brand perception.
- **No feedback loop**: Win/loss outcomes are not captured, so the same mistakes repeat.
- **Capacity limits**: Small teams can only handle a few tenders at a time, leaving revenue on the table.

## Step-by-Step Workflow

### Step 1: Create the Tender

A bid manager receives a hospital procurement RFP for surgical instruments. She opens MedStrato, clicks **New Tender**, fills in the tender name, issuer, estimated value, and submission deadline. The tender appears in the pipeline with a **Lead** stage tag.

**Time spent: 3 minutes.**

<Card title="Guide: Create a tender" icon="arrow-right" href="/guides/tenders/create-tender">
  Full walkthrough of every form field.
</Card>

### Step 2: Upload and Parse Documents

She uploads the 150-page RFP (PDF) and two amendment documents. One click on **AI Parse** extracts 52 technical parameters organized into 4 categories. The **Matched** indicator shows 0% — ready for product mapping.

**Time spent: under 1 minute** (AI processing takes seconds).

<Card title="Guide: Document upload & AI parse" icon="arrow-right" href="/guides/tenders/document-parse">
  Supported formats, parse results, and best practices.
</Card>

### Step 3: Technical Compliance

In the Technical editor, she clicks **Match Products** to auto-link her company's catalog to the 52 requirements. AI flags 4 items as **Partial** compliance. She opens **Tender Intel** and asks "What certification is required for parameter 17?" — the AI cites page 43 of the specification. She resolves all flags, then clicks **Generate Proposal** for each section.

**Time spent: 2 hours** (vs. 3–5 days manually).

<Card title="Guide: Technical editor" icon="arrow-right" href="/guides/tenders/technical-editor">
  Product matching, AI compliance, WIN assessment, and proposal generation.
</Card>

### Step 4: Commercial Pricing

The finance lead opens the Commercial tab. The quotation table is pre-built from matched products. She uses AI price suggestions on consumable lines, adjusts capital equipment margins, and adds a service fee column. The margin tool confirms 22% gross profit — within target.

**Time spent: 45 minutes.**

<Card title="Guide: Commercial editor" icon="arrow-right" href="/guides/tenders/commercial-editor">
  Quotation table, price suggestions, margin tools, and fee management.
</Card>

### Step 5: Generate and Export the Proposal

In the Tender Package tab, the team selects 11 sections (cover, TOC, executive summary, technical approach, commercial summary, and more). **Generate All** produces a complete draft. The marketing lead uses **Professional** tone adjustment on the executive summary and a custom rewrite to highlight the company's local service network. They export as PDF and finalize.

**Time spent: 30 minutes.**

<Card title="Guide: Tender Package" icon="arrow-right" href="/guides/tenders/tender-package">
  Section selection, AI generation, editing, and export.
</Card>

### Step 6: Post-Bid Debrief

Three weeks later, the tender is awarded to the team. The bid manager moves it to **Won** and fills in the debrief: "Won on technical compliance and local service commitment. Pricing was competitive but not the lowest." This data feeds into MedStrato's AI scoring for future opportunities.
