## Your Role in MedStrato

Marketing and Business Development teams use MedStrato to run multi-channel campaigns, create AI-assisted content, track market trends, and identify business opportunities.

<Info>
This guide maps your daily workflows to MedStrato features. For detailed instructions, follow the links to specific guides.
</Info>

## Your Key Workflows

### 1. Campaign Management

Create and execute email campaigns to engage KOLs and customers.

| Task | Where in MedStrato | Guide |
|------|----------------------|-------|
| Create a new campaign | **Campaigns** → **New Campaign** | [Campaigns Overview](/guides/campaign) |
| Choose campaign type | Campaign → Select type (Product Update, Newsletter, etc.) | [Campaigns Overview](/guides/campaign) |
| Build your audience | Campaign → **Audience** tab | [Campaigns Overview](/guides/campaign) |
| Select email template | Campaign → **Template** selection | [Email Templates](/guides/campaign/email-templates) |
| Personalize with variables | Campaign → Edit email content | [Personalization Variables](/guides/campaign/personalization-variables) |
| Preview and test | Campaign → **Preview** | [Preview & Test Emails](/guides/campaign/preview-and-testing) |
| Schedule or send | Campaign → **Schedule** settings | [Campaigns Overview](/guides/campaign) |
| Track performance | Campaign → **Analytics** | [Campaigns Overview](/guides/campaign) |

**Campaign types:**
- **Product Update**: Announce new features or improvements
- **Event Invitation**: Invite audience to events
- **New Product Launch**: Major product announcements
- **Newsletter**: Regular updates and content digest
- **Custom**: Flexible format for any purpose

**Email tracking metrics:**
- Delivery rate
- Open rate
- Click rate
- Unsubscribe rate
- Bounce rate

### 2. Content Creation (Press & Articles)

Generate AI-assisted marketing content.

| Task | Where in MedStrato | Guide |
|------|----------------------|-------|
| Create a new article | **Press** → **New Article** | [Create Article](/guides/press/create-article) |
| Generate with AI assistance | Article → AI content generation | [Create Article](/guides/press/create-article) |
| Refine and improve content | Article → **Improve** tools | [Improve Article](/guides/press/improve-article) |
| Add SEO keywords | Article → Keyword settings | [Improve Article](/guides/press/improve-article) |
| Publish or export | Article → Publish/Download | [Press Overview](/guides/press) |

**Content types you can create:**
- Blog articles
- Press releases
- Product announcements
- Thought leadership pieces
- Case studies

### 3. Market Intelligence

Track trends and identify opportunities.

| Task | Where in MedStrato | Guide |
|------|----------------------|-------|
| Monitor market trends | **Trends** → Dashboard | [Trends Overview](/guides/trends) |
| View funding flows by region | **Trends** → Geographic view | [Trend Details](/guides/trends/trend-details) |
| Track procurement opportunities | **Opportunities** module | — |
| Analyze competitive signals | **Signals** → Industry filter | [Signals Overview](/guides/signals) |
| Generate market research | **Investigations** → Market Analysis template | [Create Investigation](/guides/investigations/create-investigation) |

**Trend data includes:**
- Funding amounts by region
- Procurement activity
- Project timelines
- Currency conversions

### 4. KOL Engagement for Marketing

Coordinate with Medical Affairs on KOL outreach.

| Task | Where in MedStrato | Guide |
|------|----------------------|-------|
| View KOL database | **KOL** → Dashboard | [KOL Management](/guides/kol) |
| Filter KOLs for campaigns | **KOL** → Filters (tier, specialty) | [KOL Management](/guides/kol) |
| Add KOLs to campaign audience | **Campaigns** → Audience → Add KOLs | [Campaigns Overview](/guides/campaign) |
| View KOL engagement history | **KOL** → Profile → Activity | [KOL Profile](/guides/kol/kol-profile-and-insights) |

<Warning>
Coordinate with Medical Affairs before reaching out to Tier 1 KOLs to ensure messaging alignment.
</Warning>

### 5. AI-Assisted Workflows

Use MedStrato AI to accelerate marketing tasks.

| Task | How to Access | What It Does |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| Create newsletter campaign | `/campaign newsletter to [audience]` | Draft newsletter content |
| Launch product campaign | `/campaign launch [product]` | Generate product launch sequence |
| Cold outreach to KOLs | `/outreach cold to [specialty] KOLs` | Draft introduction emails |
| Follow-up with contacts | `/outreach followup` | Generate follow-up sequences |

Access these via the **AI** module or type `/` in the command bar.

## Quick Start Checklist

Get set up in MedStrato as a Marketing user:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your first campaign">
    Go to **Campaigns** → **New Campaign** and select a campaign type. [Guide](/guides/campaign)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your audience">
    Add KOLs from the database or import external contacts to your campaign audience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up email templates">
    Explore available templates and customize for your brand. [Guide](/guides/campaign/email-templates)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register your products">
    Go to **Products** → **New Product** to add your product portfolio for campaign association. [Guide](/guides/products)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore market trends">
    Go to **Trends** to see funding and procurement activity in your target markets. [Guide](/guides/trends)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Campaign Planning Calendar

MedStrato helps you plan campaigns around events and product milestones:

| Timing | Campaign Type | Audience |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| 4 weeks before event | Event Save the Date | Target attendees |
| 2 weeks before event | Event Invitation | Target attendees |
| 1 week before event | Event Reminder | Registered attendees |
| Post-event | Thank You + Content | Attendees |
| Product launch | Launch Announcement | Full list |
| Monthly | Newsletter | Subscribers |
