## What you can do

- Create investigation reports from templates.
- Track status (generating → draft → completed).
- Reopen or refresh past investigations.
- Export and share reports with stakeholders.

## Where to find it

- Open `Investigations` from the main navigation.

## How investigations are organized

- **New Investigation** starts a new run using a template and scope.
- The left panel acts as an archive of past investigations.
- Each report shows a status such as **Generating…**, **Draft**, or **Completed**.

## What’s in a report

Depending on the template, reports may include:

- Regulatory and policy changes
- Competitor intelligence
- Market opportunity signals (tenders, grants, programs)
- Sentiment and reputation insights

## Export and sharing

- Export reports to formats like PDF/Word for distribution.
- Use sharing tools to send reports to collaborators.
