## Overview

When you select a product from the list, its detail panel opens on the right side of the screen. The detail view is organized into tabs so you can quickly find the information you need.

## Your product tabs

| Tab | What's inside |
|---|---|
| **Overview** | Key details (product line, status, description), related bids, and regulatory signals |
| **Specifications** | Technical specs organized by category (dimensions, performance, materials, safety, etc.) |
| **Documents** | Upload and manage product documents with automatic version tracking |
| **Pricing** | Pricing tables and cost breakdowns |
| **Regulatory** | Certification status, clearances, and compliance information |
| **Competitors** | Linked competitor products with side-by-side feature comparisons |
| **History** | Full edit history showing who changed what and when |

## Competitor product tabs

When viewing a competitor product, you see a different set of tabs:

| Tab | What's inside |
|---|---|
| **Overview** | Competitor product details, market positioning, and key differentiators |
| **Specifications** | Technical specs as reported or researched |
| **Competitive Analysis** | AI-generated competitive analysis against your products |
| **Linked Products** | Which of your products compete with this one |

## Document management

The Documents tab lets you upload files (brochures, datasheets, certification documents) directly to a product. Each document supports version tracking:

- Upload a new version of an existing document and the system automatically increments the version number.
- View the version history to see all past uploads.
- Download any version at any time.

## Edit history

The History tab records every change made to the product — field-level diffs showing the old and new values, who made the change, and when. This is useful for audit trails and understanding how a product's profile has evolved.

<Tip>
Click the **Edit** button in the top-right of the detail panel to modify product information. Changes are saved immediately and recorded in the History tab.
</Tip>
