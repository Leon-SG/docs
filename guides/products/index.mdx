## What you can do

- Browse your full product catalog and competitor products in a unified list with search, filtering, and grouping.
- View detailed product information across seven tabs: Overview, Specifications, Documents, Pricing, Regulatory, Competitors, and History.
- Upload and version product documents (brochures, datasheets, certifications).
- Compare multiple products side by side with a specification matrix that highlights differences.
- Monitor competitive health with KPIs, heatmaps, and gap analysis.
- Track every edit with a full change history and audit trail.

## Where to find it

Open **Products** from the main sidebar. The page has two top-level tabs:

| Tab | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Products** | Browse and manage your product catalog and competitor products |
| **Compare** | Select products and compare their specifications side by side |

## Browsing products

The Products tab uses a three-column layout:

- **Left panel** — Alphabetical product list with search, type filter (All / Our Products / Competitors), and grouping options (by Name, by Product Line, by Type).
- **Right panel** — Detailed product view with tabbed navigation.

Click any product in the list to see its full details. Your products and competitor products have different tab sets tailored to the information available for each.

## What product data is used for

- **Tenders** — Match your product specs against tender requirements for bid response generation.
- **Training** — AI generates training quizzes and scenarios from your product and competitor data.
- **KOL alignment** — Match products to relevant specialties and experts.
- **Signals** — Map regulatory updates to impacted products.
- **Investigations** — Keep research reports grounded in your actual portfolio.
- **Campaigns** — Link products to outreach so recipients get the right context.
