## What you can do

- Search and filter products by category and recency.
- Track status, regulatory standing, and target markets.
- Keep product data consistent for downstream AI features and reporting.
- Add new products and manage product lines.

## Where to find it

- Open `Products` from the main navigation.

## What product data is used for

- **KOL alignment**: match products to relevant specialties and experts.
- **Signals**: map regulatory updates to impacted products.
- **Investigations**: keep research reports grounded in your actual portfolio.
- **Campaigns**: link products to outreach so recipients get the right context.
