## What you can do

- Maintain a single source of truth for registrations by market.
- Track submissions, approvals, and renewals without missing expirations.
- Search by product, authority, or registration ID.

## Where to find it

- Open `Registrations` from the main navigation.

## Status buckets

- **Active**: valid registrations you can market/distribute under.
- **Pending**: submissions under review.
- **Expiring**: registrations near end-of-validity (start renewal early).

## What each row includes

- Product + registration identifier (e.g., submission number).
- Authority/jurisdiction.
- Status + type.
- Expiration date + last updated timestamp.
