## What you can do

- Create and manage events (conferences, symposiums, meetings).
- Search and filter by timeline (upcoming vs completed).
- Track attendee RSVP status and invitation engagement.
- Open an event to manage agenda, attendees, materials, and submissions.

## Where to find it

- Open `Events` from the main navigation.

## Search and filters

- **Search**: find events by title or keyword.
- **Time filters**: focus on events happening soon (e.g., within 1 month) or review past events in **Completed**.
