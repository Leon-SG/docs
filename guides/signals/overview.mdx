**1. Overview**

The Signals module is a real-time regulatory intelligence engine. It monitors and aggregates critical updates, policy changes, and market announcements from the worldâ€™s leading healthcare and medical device regulatory authorities. This tool enables organizations to proactively manage compliance risks and identify strategic opportunities by staying ahead of the global regulatory landscape.

**2. Monitored Regulatory Sources**

The system tracks high-authority data streams from primary global regulators and industry intelligence providers, including:

- **U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)**
- **European Medicines Agency (EMA)**
- **World Health Organization (WHO)**
- **Health Canada**
- **Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency (MHRA)**
- **Regulatory Focus (RAPS)**
- **MedTech Dive**

**3. Impact Assessment and Visualization**

Every signal is processed and categorized based on its potential influence on the industry or specific product lines.

- **Geospatial Intelligence**: The heat map visualizes the geographic concentration of regulatory activity. Clusters are color-coded to represent the highest level of urgency in a specific region.
- **Impact Levels**:
  - **Critical (Red)**: Major regulatory shifts or safety alerts requiring immediate attention.
  - **High (Orange)**: Significant policy changes or new approval requirements.
  - **Medium (Yellow)**: Standard updates, pilot program news, or administrative changes.
  - **Low (Blue)**: Routine reports or general industry information.

**4. Signal Registry and Data Mapping**

The signal table provides a structured view of each intelligence item:

- **Source and Origin**: Identifies the specific regulator and the country of origin.
- **Contextual Intelligence**: Provides the title and a concise summary of the regulatory update.
- **Product Alignment**: A key feature that automatically links regulatory signals to specific medical devices in your portfolio (e.g., "AnesThesia X9"), allowing users to immediately see which products are affected by a specific update.
- **Sentiment and Timing**: Captures the tone of the update and the official publication date to ensure historical tracking.

**5. Advanced Filtering and Retrieval**

To navigate the high volume of global signals, users can refine the feed using several parameters:

- **Source Filter**: Isolate updates from specific authorities like the FDA or EMA.
- **Impact and Sentiment Filters**: Focus on critical risks or positive market developments.
- **Product Filter**: View only the regulatory changes that directly impact selected product lines.
- **Keyword Search**: Search for specific technical terms, device categories, or legislative acts.
