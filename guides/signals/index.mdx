## What you can do

- Track regulatory and market updates from key sources.
- Prioritize signals by impact level and sentiment.
- See which products may be affected (automatic mapping).
- Save, share, and open a signal for deeper analysis.

## Where to find it

- Open `Signals` from the main navigation.

## Sources (examples)

Signals can include updates from regulators and industry intelligence providers such as FDA, EMA, WHO, Health Canada, MHRA, RAPS, and MedTech Dive.

## How to prioritize signals (fast path)

1. Filter to **Critical/High** impact.
2. Filter by product line or market if you have a focus area.
3. Open the signal and review **Related Products** and **Potential Impact**.

## Filters and fields

Common filters include:

- Source (authority/provider)
- Impact and sentiment
- Product mapping
- Keyword search
