## What you can do

- **Take knowledge quizzes** — AI generates questions from your tenders, products, competitors, and institutions so your team stays sharp on real data.
- **Practice with voice coaching** — Role-play realistic customer scenarios (bid defense, competitive battles, technical Q&A, price negotiations) with an AI coach.
- **Review flashcards** — Spaced-repetition cards that adapt to each person's learning pace, covering product specs, competitor differentiators, and compliance details.
- **Track daily streaks** — A new challenge appears every day to build consistent learning habits.
- **Monitor team performance** — Capability radar charts, leaderboards, and AI-recommended bid teams based on individual strengths.

## Where to find it

Open **Training** from the main sidebar navigation.

<Tip>
Training is available to all users with the Training module enabled. Ask your admin to turn it on in Settings if you don't see it.
</Tip>

## How it works

Training Hub pulls from data already in your workspace — products, tenders, competitor analysis, and institution records. The AI uses this real context to generate relevant questions and scenarios, so training is always tied to your actual business.

| Training format | Best for | Time per session |
|---|---|---|
| Knowledge Quiz | Quick knowledge checks on specs, compliance, market data | 3–5 min |
| Voice Coaching | Practicing customer conversations and objection handling | 5–10 min |
| Flashcards | Long-term retention of key facts and differentiators | 2–5 min |
| Daily Challenge | Maintaining a learning streak with one question per day | 1 min |

## Capability tracking

Every session contributes to your personal capability radar, which measures five dimensions:

- **Product Knowledge** — How well you know your own portfolio
- **Competitive Analysis** — Understanding of competitor strengths and weaknesses
- **Parameter Matching** — Ability to map specs to tender requirements
- **Compliance Understanding** — Regulatory and certification knowledge
- **Market Insight** — Awareness of market trends and regional dynamics

Your scores update automatically as you complete sessions. Managers can view team-wide capabilities to identify training gaps and recommend members for specific bids.
