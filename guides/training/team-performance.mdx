## Overview

Training Hub tracks individual and team-level capabilities so managers can identify strengths, gaps, and the best people for each bid.

## Capability radar

Each team member has a five-axis capability radar built from their training history:

- **Product Knowledge**
- **Competitive Analysis**
- **Parameter Matching**
- **Compliance Understanding**
- **Market Insight**

Scores range from 0 to 100 and update after every training session. The radar chart is visible on each person's profile and in the team dashboard.

## Leaderboard

The leaderboard ranks team members by overall training score. It shows:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Rank** | Position based on overall score |
| **Name** | Team member |
| **Overall score** | Weighted average across all capability dimensions |
| **Quiz score** | Average quiz performance |
| **Sim score** | Average voice coaching evaluation |
| **Streak** | Consecutive days with at least one completed session |

## AI-recommended bid teams

When preparing for a bid, the AI can recommend the best team composition based on:

- The tender's technical requirements
- Each person's capability profile and training history
- Coverage across all five capability dimensions

This helps ensure your bid team has the right mix of product knowledge, competitive awareness, and compliance expertise.

## Where to find it

Open **Training** → scroll to the team section, or navigate to **Training > Team** from the sidebar.

<Tip>
Team performance data is only visible to users with manager-level permissions. Individual users can always see their own capability radar and session history.
</Tip>
