# Document Upload & AI Parse

Once your tender is created, upload the source documents and let AI break them into structured, actionable requirements.

> **Scenario:** A procurement team receives a 200-page hospital RFP. They upload the PDF, run AI parse, and within seconds have 47 structured parameters organized into categories — ready for product matching in the Technical editor.

## Upload documents

Open your tender and go to the **Files** tab. Upload the RFP, technical specifications, or any supporting documents. Supported formats include **PDF** and **DOCX**.

![Upload technical documents](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/03-upload-docs.jpeg)

## Run AI parse

After uploading, click **AI Parse** (or the equivalent button) to start extraction.

![Parse step: AI parse](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/04-parse-ai.jpeg)

The parser reads through the documents and extracts:

- **Categories** — Logical groupings of requirements (e.g., equipment specs, service terms).
- **Parameters** — Individual requirements within each category.
- **Matched %** — Shows how many requirements are linked to your products (starts at 0%, updates as you match in the Technical editor).

![After AI parse: categories and parameters](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/05-after-parse.jpeg)

## What happens next

From the parse results screen, you can enter the **Technical editor** to begin matching products to requirements.

<Tip>
For best results, upload the most detailed technical specification document first. The AI parser prioritizes the primary document for requirement extraction.
</Tip>

## More scenarios

> **Multi-document tender:** A government RFP arrives as six separate files — technical specs, administrative requirements, pricing template, evaluation criteria, draft contract, and amendments. The bid team uploads all six. AI parse consolidates requirements across documents into a single structured view, flagging where amendments override original specs.

> **Tight deadline:** An urgent tender lands on Friday afternoon with a Monday submission deadline. The compliance lead uploads the 90-page PDF, runs AI parse, and has structured requirements ready in under a minute — leaving the weekend for technical and commercial work instead of manual extraction.
