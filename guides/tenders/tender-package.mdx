# Tender Package

The Tender Package tab produces your final submission document — cover page, executive summary, technical approach, commercial summary, and more. AI generates the content; you refine and export.

> **Scenario:** A bid team selects 11 sections for their proposal, clicks **Generate All**, and has a complete draft in minutes. The manager uses AI edit to shorten the executive summary, makes the technical approach more professional, and exports a polished PDF for submission.

## Open the Tender Package

From the Commercial editor, click **Go to Tender Package**, or open the tender's **Package** tab.

![Tender Package overview](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/04-tender-package.jpeg)

## Select sections

The left panel lists available sections:

- Cover page
- Table of contents
- Executive Summary
- Technical Approach
- Commercial Summary
- Additional sections as configured

Use checkboxes to **include** or **exclude** sections from the generated document.

![Select sections and generate](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/21-package-generate.jpeg)

## Generate content

Click **Generate All** to run AI generation across all selected sections. Progress is shown (e.g., "Generating 0/11…"). A **DRAFT** watermark appears while generation is in progress.

![Full AI generation](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/24-package-ai-full.jpeg)

## Refine with AI edit

Click any section to open the AI edit panel on the right. Available actions:

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Shorter** | Condense the text |
| **Longer** | Expand with more detail |
| **Professional** | Adjust to formal business tone |
| **Rephrase** | Rewrite for clarity |
| **Custom rewrite** | Enter your own instruction and click **Rewrite** |

![AI edit panel](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/22-package-ai-edit.jpeg)

## Export and finalize

When the document is ready:

- **Export DOCX** — download as a Word document for further editing.
- **Export PDF** — download as a PDF for submission.
- **Finalize** — lock the document and remove the draft watermark. A confirmation dialog appears; once confirmed, further edits may be restricted.

![Export options](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/23-package-export.jpeg)

<Tip>
Export as DOCX first if your team needs a final review pass. Use **Finalize** only when the document is submission-ready.
</Tip>

## More scenarios

> **Brand consistency:** A marketing manager reviews the generated executive summary and uses the **Professional** tone adjustment to align it with the company's brand voice. She then adds a custom rewrite instruction: "Emphasize our 15-year track record in surgical robotics" — the AI incorporates it seamlessly.

> **Last-minute amendment:** The buyer issues an amendment 48 hours before deadline, changing two technical requirements. The bid team updates the Technical editor, regenerates the affected Tender Package sections, and exports a revised PDF — without rebuilding the entire document from scratch.
