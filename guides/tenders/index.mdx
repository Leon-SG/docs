## The bid lifecycle at a glance

Every tender follows a clear path:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create">
    Set up the tender with metadata, timelines, and financials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Parse">
    Upload RFP documents and let AI extract categories and requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Technical compliance">
    Match products to requirements, verify compliance, and generate proposal text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Commercial pricing">
    Build the quotation with margin tools, price suggestions, and fee management.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Package & export">
    Generate a professional proposal document. Refine with AI, then export as DOCX or PDF.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Debrief & learn">
    Record win/loss outcomes and reasons. The system uses this feedback to improve future bid scoring.
  </Step>
</Steps>
