# Commercial Editor

The Commercial editor turns your technical matches into a priced quotation. Use built-in margin tools and AI price suggestions to build competitive, profitable bids.

> **Scenario:** After completing technical compliance, the finance lead opens the Commercial tab. The quotation table is pre-populated from matched products. She uses the margin tools to optimize pricing, applies a volume discount, and gets the gross profit to target — all before the submission deadline.

## Open the Commercial editor

From the Technical editor, click **Go to Commercial**, or open the tender's **Commercial** tab directly.

![Commercial editor overview](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/03b-commercial-editor.jpeg)

## Quotation table

The table is auto-built from products matched in the Technical editor.

**Default columns:**

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Qty** | Quantity required |
| **Cost price** | Your internal cost |
| **Unit price** | Price offered to the buyer |
| **Discount** | Discount percentage or amount |
| **Tax** | Applicable tax rate |
| **Totals** | Line total |
| **Margin** | Profit margin per line |
| **Note** | Internal notes |

![Quotation table](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/15-commercial-overview.jpeg)

## Price suggestions

Hover over a price cell to see AI-powered suggestions (e.g., historical prices, "DB Price"). Click to apply.

![Price suggestions](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/16-price-suggest.png)

## Margin and profitability tools

Use the top bar or sidebar for:

- **Gross profit summary** — real-time profitability overview.
- **Target margin** — set a margin goal and see which lines need adjustment.

![Margin tools](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/17-margin-tools.jpeg)

## Add rows and columns

- **+ Add Row** — add extra line items (e.g., services, accessories).
- **+ Add Column** — add fee columns or custom fields.

![Add row](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/18-add-row.jpeg)

![Add fee column](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/19-add-fee.jpeg)

## More scenarios

> **Margin pressure:** A sales director reviews a tender where the buyer's budget is tight. She uses the margin tools to identify three line items where switching to a cost-effective alternative still meets compliance. The adjusted quote hits the target price while preserving 18% gross margin.

> **Multi-lot pricing:** A large hospital group issues a tender with four lots across different departments. The bid team adds separate rows and fee columns for each lot, using price suggestions to stay competitive on high-volume consumables while protecting margin on capital equipment.

## Move to Tender Package

When pricing is finalized, click **Go to Tender Package** to generate the proposal document.

![Go to Tender Package](/images/user-manual/5-1-tenders/20-to-package.jpeg)
