## Operating Regions

MedStrato is offered in different regions through the following entities. For sales, support, or demo requests, please contact the operator for your region.

---

### Global

**Galaxias Inc.** operates MedStrato globally (excluding the regions below).

- **Website**: [galaxiasinc.com](https://galaxiasinc.com)
- **Scope**: Americas, Europe, and other markets

---

### Greater China & Southeast Asia

**Adventurer Limited** is responsible for sales and operations in:

- China (mainland)
- Hong Kong
- Macau
- Southeast Asia

- **Website**: [adventurer.fun](https://adventurer.fun/)
- For inquiries, demos, or support in these regions, please reach out via the link above.
